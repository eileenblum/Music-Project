Unleash the beast within
feel the need for blood
taste the warmth of flesh
and fight to survive
Life is war!

Go back in time
for human nature
find the roots
of yourself!

Anger out of nothingness
The cold of the ancient times
The epoch of the pure

Dark nights of betrayal
alightened by the grimson moon

Cruelty with no mercy
for there shall be no peace!
