voveso in mori mon vandas.
veidos ventos cambon mon verno
et con papon lomnen
luxtodos imon siraxta.
suouelo, imon oro
suouelo, tauson tunda
voveso in mori mon vandas

[English translation:]

In the ocean of my tears I drown.
Howling winds are bending my mast
and my longing deepens with every dram.

Welcome, my shore
Welcome, silent wave
In the ocean of my tears I drown.
