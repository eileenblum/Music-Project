Lady
Power of the night
Allied with dead sound
With neither echoes nor resonances
Unconquered and eager for death

Exhort beauty, exhale
Insatiable lady, abominable

She
Shadows, vomiting darkness
Shadows, smells sickening
Shadows, rot black mist

Eager for bastard herds
Lady of the abyss

Lady... allied with rotter, companion
With faith, without power
Destined to her
Sow corpses, lady, harvest plagues, lady,
Collect deceased, lady

Possessor of infected tumulus,
Domineering of old bastards
Possessor of throne without life,
Domineering of lands in shadows
Lady of the abyss,
I love her, I want her
