Howl to the moons of affront!
Bear schism against the open gates!
The revealed way!

Lexiphanic levitations
In adoration of false spirit
Nursing the swollen death
Of lost spirit
False spirit!

Dilutions of merit
Chronicling excreta
Beguiling distinction
Burning progressions

Making enemies
Named champions

Banners of strife
Crying pity!
For nothing in return!

Equipollence embraced
To defeat of liberations
