Crying out.
Seeping down.
Torn away, apart, alone.
Clothes unwashed.
Body numb.
Words repeat in jagged circles.
Drained of hope.
Strength is gone.
Eyes are fixed upon the ground.
Chased by pain.
Loss pervades.
Frantic dark.
Beg for rest.
Withdrawal.
