Allelujah eleison
Jesu Christe
Morte et dabo

Dream deep of phantoms and gloom
Watch the night invade every room
A distant tower beckons us all
And fills the air with restless warning

Cold hearted souls reveal the night
Reflecting sorrow in our sight
As death awakens those who dream
Catherdral bells toll for thee
