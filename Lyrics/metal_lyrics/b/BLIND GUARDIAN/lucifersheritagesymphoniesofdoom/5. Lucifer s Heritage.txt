Blind Guardian/Lucifer's Heritage - Symphonies of Doom.
Demo album, self-released, 1985.

Total playing time: 18:20.

LUCIFER'S HERITAGE are:
Hansi Kursch - bass, lead vocals.
Andre Olbrich - guitar.

This is the first demo album of "Lucifer's Heritage", who later changed their name to Blind Guardian.
