In your glance... the sensuality
In your eyes... there is the fatality

Sinuous
Voluptuous
Disturbing
You are... you are
Terrible
Deadly
Glacial
You are... you are
Theda Bara! Theda Bara!

Engage with your silence
Those frames in black and white
Did you express sorrow and love
Hope and horror

In the embrace of death
Show all the anguish
The evil in your eyes
The evil in your movements

Sinuous
Voluptuous
Disturbing...

The essence of evil
stamped on the screen
Rest upset
Unique and divine
