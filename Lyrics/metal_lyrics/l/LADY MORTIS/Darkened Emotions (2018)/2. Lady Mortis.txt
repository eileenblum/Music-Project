Fallen
An eternal memory
Blackened heart
In my pain
A love for all the times
Nevermore will back to this life
Eternal beloved

Lady Mortis
Lady of my suffer

My tears
Run down red
Possessed
In eyes that be lost
Between memories and day dreams
Longing for the sin of my devotion
