The acquisition of knowledge is the pinnacle of human existence. The dismissal of ones ability is a great misfortune. The acceptance of the human condition is to remain stagnant. To embrace materialism is to accept neutralism. Separation of three-dimensional forms is to embrace the ideals of a long forgotten virtue: freedom. To engage in the intellect is to encompass all that is beautiful. Ignorance is the very crux of a society in which social norms are instilled to prevent the expansion of ideas. Sentience is the union of outlook and the limitless if we chose to demand consciousness for the comatose. This is of great importance for the future is apprehensible.
