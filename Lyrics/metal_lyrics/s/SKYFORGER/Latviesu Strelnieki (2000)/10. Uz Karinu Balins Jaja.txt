[Brother Rides To The War]

Latviesu Strelnieki was recorded and mixed at Phoenix Recording,
Riga, Latvia, during autumn-winter of 1999
Produced by Lundberg &amp; SKYFORGER
Released on October 9, 2000

The album is about World War I and the regiments of Latvian riflemen,
which defended the land of our forefathers against the aggressors of
Imperial Germany.
