On the first day I will destroy the beasts of the earth.
On the second day I will destroy the birds of the air and the fishes of the sea.
On the third day I will destroy the lights of heaven, including the sun, moon and the stars.
On the fourth day I will destroy land, called Earth, endowed with plants, flowers and trees.
On the fifth day I will destroy the firmament, which He called Heaven.
Finally, on the sixth day I will destroy light thus Day and Night.
