This is the underworld,
the place where the dark emperor
creates the demonic horde of hate.
With torture and torment,
they get their endless life
to slaughter the bastards of the light.
Surrounded by screaming souls of suffering.
They are damned for centuries
to serve their master,
the tyrannical King of hell.
This is the majestic palace
of death and immortal beasts
THIS IS HELL.
